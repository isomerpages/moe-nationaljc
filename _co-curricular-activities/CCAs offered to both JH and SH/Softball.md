<h3>Softball</h3>

<p>The NJC Softball Team strives to cultivate the best team players who play
with aggression, commitment and discipline. As a team, we will support
and fight together to achieve excellence. The NJC Softball team lives by
the motto: Scoring homeruns in all fields of life!</p>
<p>NJC softball believes in guiding and nurturing individuals into competent,
skillful and courageous softball players. Off the field, NJC softball players
have fire in their bellies, supportive of their peers and most of all,
lives by the College values of Civility, Altruism, Enterprise and Resillience.
NJC Softball Team comprises of JH Girls and Boys Teams and SH Boys and
Girls Teams.</p>
