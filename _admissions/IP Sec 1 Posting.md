<h3>Integrated Programme (Sec 1 Posting)</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:99%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/ad1.png">
</div>
<p>The Secondary One Posting Exercise is conducted by the Ministry of Education
(MOE) to post Primary 6 pupils who have passed the Primary School Leaving
Examinations (PSLE) to a secondary school based on their PSLE results and
their school choices.</p>
<p>Students admitted to our College under this exercise will be placed in
our 6-year <a href="https://www.nationaljc.moe.edu.sg/junior-high/integrated-programme/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Integrated Programme</a>.</p>
<p>For more information on the Secondary One Posting Exercise, please refer
to this <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/secondary/s1-posting" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MOE Website</a>.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Students who are Posted to NJC for 2025: S1 Registration</u></strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Congratulations on the posting of your child to National Junior College!</p>
<p></p>
<p>We will <strong>communicate with you via Parents Gateway (PG) from the afternoon of the S1 Posting Results Release date</strong> (tentatively
between 18 Dec 2024 to 20 Dec 2024). Please check your PG mobile application
for important information and instructions, including an <strong>online&nbsp;Student Registration Form that should be submitted as soon as possible</strong> or
by&nbsp;11 am on the day after the release of S1 Posting results<strong>.</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Please note that there is <strong>no need to visit the school in person for the S1 Registration</strong>.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The purchase of school uniform and books can be done online, or at NJC
on <strong>26 and 27 Dec 2024 (Thu and Fri)</strong>; details will be provided
in the PG notification as well.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Please note that the <strong>school term begins on 13 Jan 2025 (Mon)</strong> for
National Junior College.</p>
<p></p>
<p>If you are not already familiar with PG, you may visit <a href="http://pg.moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">http://pg.moe.edu.sg</a> for
more information. Links to download the PG mobile application are also
found on the PG website.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Should you have any queries, or if you are unable to access PG, please
contact the College at 64661144 or email us at <a href="mailto:njc@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">njc@moe.edu.sg</a>.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Appeals for admission to NJC IP Junior High 1 2025</u></strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Appeals for admission to NJC IP Junior High 1 for 2025 will open on the
day of release of S1 Posting results and close after 5 calendar days at
10am.</p>
<p>Only official online appeals within the appeal period, complete with supporting
documents, will be considered. Late appeals will not be entertained.</p>
<p>Successful appellants will be informed of the appeal outcome by <strong>6 Jan 2025 (Mon)</strong>.<strong> </strong>If
you do not hear from the school by this date, the appeal is deemed to be
unsuccessful.</p>
