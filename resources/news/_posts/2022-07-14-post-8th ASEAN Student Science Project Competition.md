#### 8th ASEAN Student Science Project Competition

Eunice Ho (21SH12) and Jay Tan (21SH08) were members of a Singapore team that participated in the 8th ASEAN Student Science Project Competition. Jay clinched third place in the Full Experimental Project Applied Sciences category and Eunice won the Project of the Year Award.
